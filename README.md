---

### 🕷️ THE ANATOMY OF CHAOS
This isn't just HTML/CSS. This is a canvas of digital vandalism built on raw frontend mathematics. 

* **🩸 The Blood Sacrament Engine:** A custom HTML5 Canvas drawing board. It uses Bezier curve smoothing and velocity-based stroke thickness so the ink physically "bleeds" when you draw fast. 
* **🪦 Madman's Confinement:** A custom stop-motion background generator. It renders erratic, aggressive charcoal scratches at a locked **8fps** to induce a restless, claustrophobic atmosphere.
* **🔪 Razor Slash Navigation:** GSAP-powered timelines combined with complex SVG `clip-paths` to simulate a razor blade slicing the screen and tearing the paper when you click a link.
* **📎 Heavy Hardware Physics:** Zero bloated libraries. Every silver safety pin, ripped washi tape, and swinging voodoo doll is powered by pure CSS and Vanilla JS physics.
